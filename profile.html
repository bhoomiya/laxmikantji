Biography

Cultivating the Future

I am Dr. Lakshmikant Pandey, widely known as SEEDMAN®, an agribusiness professional with over—35 years of experience in the Indian seed industry. With a strong academic foundation—PhD in Agricultural Sciences, MSc in Seed Technology, and MBA—my work focuses on integrating science, strategy, and sustainability to strengthen agricultural value chains and farmer livelihoods.

My career has been dedicated to transforming agriculture from a subsistence activity into a market-driven, profitable agri-business ecosystem, with seeds as the foundation of productivity, resilience, and food security.

Professional Path

My Journey

Ananya Seeds Pvt. Ltd.

Founder & MD (2014 – Present)

Founder & Managing Director

Built Ananya Seeds into a recognized Indian seed brand with a portfolio spanning vegetable and field crop seeds, multi-location operations (Delhi, Bengaluru, Haryana), and a focus on climate-resilient, high-yield hybrids that improve farmer returns. Annual turnover scaled to ₹5–25 Crores; direct and indirect impact on thousands of farmers through improved seed access.

Mahyco Seeds Ltd.

General Manager – Government Sales (2012 – 2013)

Led large-scale institutional and government seed programs, managing high-volume, time-bound supply contracts with strict compliance; strengthened public-sector relationships and ensured quality delivery across multiple regions and crop seasons.

Sungro Seeds Ltd.

National Business Manager (2005 – 2012)

Headed pan-India business operations across diverse crop portfolios; managed national distribution and marketing networks and improved production-to-market alignment across agro-climatic zones, playing a key role in scaling Sungro’s national footprint.

Sungro Seeds Ltd.

General Manager – Production (1997 – 2005)

Led hybrid seed production, processing, and quality systems; standardized production protocols across multiple locations, improving yield consistency, quality assurance, and supply reliability.

Mahyco Seeds Ltd.

Management Trainee → Regional Leadership (1990 – 1997)

Developed hands-on expertise across field operations, production, and quality; worked closely with farmers and technical teams, building a foundational understanding of end-to-end seed value chains and progressing into leadership roles.
